The upgrade plan

A focused redesign and conversion system, not a vague rebuild.

01

Premium UI pass

Modernise visual hierarchy, typography, spacing, product cards, collection pages and mobile purchase flow.

02

Trust architecture

Elevate reviews, founder story, preschool teacher credibility, retailer proof, family impact and guarantees.

03

Conversion mechanics

Bundle selector, age/need pathways, stronger CTAs, sticky mobile actions, clearer discounts and comparison blocks.

04

Performance polish

Improve mobile speed, compress media, reduce scripts, refine tracking, schema and internal linking.

⭐

Testimonials as the sales engine

Move the best stories into the buying journey: speech delay, preschool readiness, screen-free learning and grandparent gifting. These are not reviews. They are reasons to buy.

🎯

Age-based journeys

Parents do not shop by SKU. They shop by child age, problem and outcome. Build pathways like “18 months”, “speech support”, “preschool prep” and “screen-free playroom”.

🤖

AI/search clarity

Add structured FAQs, product schema, review schema, learning-outcome copy and clean semantic HTML so Google and AI answer engines understand the brand.

The aim is simple: make Hadley feel like the safest, smartest choice.

Not cheaper than Etsy. Not more generic than Target. A trusted learning brand with clearer proof, better design, and a buying journey built around parents.
